About Us

Welcome to the Text-to-Speech Conversion Application!

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a convenient and efficient solution for converting text into speech in various languages, catering to diverse user needs.

About the Project

This project is a part of a one-month online internship program where participants were tasked with developing innovative applications to address real-world challenges. The Text-to-Speech Conversion Application was conceptualized and developed as a solution to provide users with a simple yet powerful tool for converting text into speech.

Technology Used

The application utilizes the Text-to-Speech (TTS) API provided by Voice RSS, a leading provider of text-to-speech solutions. Leveraging this API, our application offers seamless conversion of text into speech in multiple languages, enhancing accessibility and user experience.

Our Team

This project was developed by a team of passionate individuals dedicated to creating impactful solutions. Our team consists of motivated developers with a shared vision of leveraging technology to make a difference.
